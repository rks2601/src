About Gajpati:
Puri is a city and a municipality in the state of Odisha, India. It is located on the Bay of Bengal, 60 kilometers south of the state capital of Bhubaneswar. Puri is known for its beautiful beaches, temples, and rich cultural heritage. The city is home to the famous Jagannath Temple, one of the four holiest places in Hinduism. The temple is dedicated to Lord Jagannath, an incarnation of Lord Vishnu, and is visited by thousands of pilgrims every year. The temple is also famous for its annual Rath Yatra, or chariot festival, when the three deities are taken out in a procession on large, intricately decorated chariots.
